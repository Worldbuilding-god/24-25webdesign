Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp cream

  1. Melt butter in frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs in the bowl
  3. Once butter is melted add eggs and cream
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan
  5. When eggs are still moist, remove from heat
